About Rudhir

Our mission is to bridge the gap between blood donors and recipients.

Our Mission

To ensure that no one suffers due to blood shortage by creating an efficient ecosystem of donors, blood banks and recipients.

Our Team

A passionate team of developers, health professionals, and volunteers working together to save lives.

Our Impact

Over 10,000 units of blood supplied, 5,000 volunteer donors registered, and countless lives saved since our inception.
